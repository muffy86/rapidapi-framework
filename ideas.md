# Design Philosophy: Neo-Brutalism

**Core Principles:**
- Brutalist foundations with vibrant color
- Thick borders and hard shadows
- Playful, energetic aesthetic
- Bold typography and asymmetric layouts

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: Bright Yellow `#FFD700`
- Accent: Hot Pink `#FF1493`
- Background: Off-white `#F5F5F5`
- Text: Pure Black `#000000`
- Borders: Black `#000000` (2-4px thick)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Space Grotesk Bold (uppercase for impact)
- Body: Inter Medium (readable, modern)
- Code: JetBrains Mono (monospace, technical)

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick black borders (3-4px) on all cards and buttons
- Hard box shadows (offset 6-8px, no blur)
- Tilted elements (rotate 1-2 degrees)
- Bright accent colors for CTAs and highlights
- Raw, unpolished aesthetic

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Bold hover states with shadow shifts
- No smooth transitions (instant changes)
- Clickable elements have strong visual feedback
- Energetic, youthful vibe

**Animation:**
- Minimal animations (prefer instant state changes)
- Shadow position shifts on hover
- Slight rotation changes for playfulness
- No fades or gradients
